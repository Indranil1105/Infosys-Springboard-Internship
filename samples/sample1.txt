# Indian Philosophy

**Problem Statement:** Indian philosophy comprises a vast and diverse intellectual tradition, often oversimplified or misunderstood in global discourse. A comprehensive study is needed to delineate its major schools, their interconnections, and contemporary relevance.

### Key Questions
- What are the principal schools of Indian philosophy and their core doctrines?
- How have these schools evolved and interacted historically?
- What is the contemporary relevance and application of Indian philosophical thought?

### Method Brief
- Conduct a comprehensive literature review of primary Sanskrit texts (in translation) and scholarly secondary sources.
- Perform comparative analysis of key concepts across different philosophical schools (e.g., epistemology, metaphysics, ethics).
- Trace the historical development and influence of major philosophical traditions.

### Deliverables
- Annotated bibliography of essential texts and scholarly articles.
- Analytical essay on a specific school or comparative concept.
- Presentation summarizing key findings and historical overview.
